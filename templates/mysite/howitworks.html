How It Works


Step 0: Buy the Book(s)


     

Purchase the CollegeBoard book "The Official SAT Study Guide Second Edition" here or the Kaplan book "Kaplan SAT 2015 Strategies, Practice and Review with 5 Practice Tests" here.  Excelerate Test Prep is not affiliated with CollegeBoard or Kaplan, but simply acts as an analysis tool for third party test preparation books.


Step 1: Choose a Test to Take


Choose a book and select a test to take. Each option for the download will correspond to a test in the book. For example "CB1" corresponds to the first CollegeBoard book practice test. You have the option in our test center to either take a full length practice test or just do random practice problems of your choice.


Step 2: Fill in the Bubble Sheet


Fill in the bubblesheet accordingly with your answers. Enter an essay score estimate from 2 to 12 and follow the instructions on the bubble sheet for the math fill in questions. Once you have finished submit the bubblesheet. If you would like to proctor or time yourself take advantage of our built-in stopwatch!


Step 3: View the Reports


After you save test data and input it refer to the many different reports we offer for a comprehensive and in depth look at your performance. Use data from any glaring vulnerabilities and focus on studying those! Click on the book icons in advanced report to get free practice problems and study material. Chart your progress and improvements over time and see yourself improve.
