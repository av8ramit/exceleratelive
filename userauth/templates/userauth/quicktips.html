Quick Tips



Tip 1: Pre-test Preparation.

Take at least one full practice test the week before your SAT. It will help you understand how to pace yourself, build your concentration endurance, and keep you relaxed for the real test. Make sure you are capable of finishing each section within the time allotted. Remember that guessing incorrectly will reduce your score so make sure you can narrow down your options to at most 3.


Tip 2: Avoiding the experimental section.

Did you know there is a way to avoid the experimental section? When two sections of the same type occur back to back one of them is guaranteed to be an experimental section. Compare that one with the ones you have seen before or see in the future and you can deduce which section is experimental. Beware: Use this tip at your own risk!
